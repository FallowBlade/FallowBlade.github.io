About Me

Hi there! I'm Izzy. I'm a She Coder with the 2022 #Plus cohort. I signed up for She Codes as I’m looking to pursue a career-pivot to tech. I’m more of a “brick builder” than a designer, so my focus for this project is on semantic HTML principles more than anything (well, as much as I can understand this to be in the last few weeks!). I’m interested in the intersect between business and technology – I thrive on solving problems related to business processes - with a special focus on bringing people (the workforce) along for the ride. For the future of tech, I strongly believe that no person should be left behind.
